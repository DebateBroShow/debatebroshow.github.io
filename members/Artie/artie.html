About Me

The lovely and eccentric producer of our show.
Neuroscience student and researcher with experience in political organizing, she is responsible for overseeing the behind-the-scenes action, maintaining the podcast’s quality of information and the group’s morale. Forged in marxist theory and sharpened by neuroscience, Artie is the brains behind the operation. With minutious research and quick fact checking she ensures our bros are always factually and metaphysically correct. Besides being rigid she’s always ready to listen and bring unique points of view into the discussion. During free time she watches anime and plays card games. Her love for data is only matched by her love for tea and plush toys.
